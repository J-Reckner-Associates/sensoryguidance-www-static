Project\nOverload<\/strong>: \nWith an active R&D team, there can be times when there are too many\nprojects in the pipeline for the client-site sensory panel. Since it doesn\u2019t\nalways make sense to expand that panel, our clients use our sensory panel to\nfill in.  We\u2019re agile and flexible, so we turn around projects quickly and\nwe work within our client\u2019s established protocols and lexicon requirements.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Protocol\nDevelopment<\/strong>: \nSometimes clients have a new product or product category that needs to be evaluated\nbut are unsure how to go about testing it.  With 20 years\u2019 experience in\nsensory research, we leverage our protocol development expertise from adjacent\nor specialized categories to develop new protocols that appropriately measure\nthese unique categories or product characteristics. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Lexicon\nDevelopment<\/strong>:\nWe\u2019ve conducted lexicon development for food, beverage, personal care,\nhousehold care, even fabric product categories.  Our proprietary lexicon\ndevelopment process is scientific while also being innovative.  We combine\nsensory principles with creative tools for a comprehensive language\nfoundation.  <\/p>\n\n\n\n
