We recently explored how gender and color impact product perceptions for laundry detergent.<\/p>\n

Interestingly, we discovered that the main effect was scent-related but that there was a two-way interaction between gender and color.\u00a0 Although women’s attitudes were not different from men’s for the clear or blue detergent, they were more negative than those of men for the green detergent.<\/p>\n
