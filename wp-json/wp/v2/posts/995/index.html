You’re probably familiar with our standing descriptive and discrimination panels (if not, click here<\/a>), but did you know that we also offer semi-trained consumer panels and in-context panels? Lately, these panels are very much in demand as manufacturers seek deeper insights into product usage and experiences. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

In-context Sensory Panels<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
  • These are sensory acute and articulate panelists<\/li>
  • The ISR panel encompasses 20 panel members with a minimum of seven years’ experience<\/li>
  • Trained to objectively evaluate attribute intensities without judging the product on liking (as opposed to consumer tests)<\/li>
  • Able to complete sensory evaluations in context<\/li>
  • Useful for receiving technical feedback as well as experiential feedback<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
