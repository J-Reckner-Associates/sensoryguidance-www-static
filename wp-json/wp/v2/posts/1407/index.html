Background<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Our client has moved their vitamin production. Based on the team tasting, the new flavors are considered an improvement from the original flavors. As such, the team would like to add a claim on the product to indicate that the taste has improved.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Objective<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The study objective is to substantiate the following claim: \u201cImproved Taste\u201d or \u201cNew Improved Taste\u201d, using prototypes with new flavors for all vitamins.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What We Did<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We conducted Descriptive Analysis with our panel of sensory experts with data collecting using a t-test.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\n
\n
  • Samples were presented using sequential monadic design and blocked by flavor.<\/li>
  • Panelists focused on flavor attributes, including acidity, tartness, and flavor character.<\/li><\/ul>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n
